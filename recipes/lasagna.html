Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe will show you how to make an easy, simple and delicious Lasagna!

You can guarantee that this Lasagna will put you in the spotlight!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion
  • 0.5kg Lean Ground Beef
  • 3/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • A whole load of Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat the oven
  2. Heat olive oil in skillet and cook onion
  3. Add beef and spices
  4. Add marinara sauce
